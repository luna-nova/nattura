WORLD OF NATTURA

Nattura is an nature-themed, Bug's Life™ inspired Dungeons and Dragons campaign. Imagine your backyard eco-system fighting each other with swords and magic but with in-depth lore with species tension, politics, and fantasy storylines.

This wiki is meant to be a hub of all the info needed to start a campaign of your own. You can follow a preset storyline or choose an time period and create your scenario for your players to play in. As a creative piece of work, Nattura is meant to challenge modern day ideals and social contructs with no black and white perspective. Every problem has far too many solutions that not everybody will agree with. The skeleton of this campaign can be your own narrative to whatever statement you are trying to make. All seriousness aside, make sure to have fun with it!
