The History Of Nattura

Antenos Dagwyr

There was a time before Ohn Pridos (Our First Pride) but it is only remembered in history as the 'Antenos Dagwyr' or Ancient Days. Small tribes scattered the land and the only means of keeping history was through stories by the fire. It was this time that the Spiders were the most dangerous group of people around.

O'hn Pridos (0 - 350)

The era of O'hn came to be when the first King Protea came upon and recieved the Mana blessing of the World Tree. This symbolized the start of the Plant Kingdom and the first organized society on a large scale. Summers were long and winters seem to be over in a blink of an eye. The World Tree seemed to rejoin in seeing all life living and flourishing.
